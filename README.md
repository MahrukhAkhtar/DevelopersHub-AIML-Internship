Summary of Findings-
There are 3 species: Setosa, Versicolor, and Virginica.
Setosa has noticeably smaller petals than the other two species.
Setosa petal_length ≈ 1–2 cm
Versicolor ≈ 3–5 cm
Virginica ≈ 4.5–7 cm
The scatter plot shows that Setosa is clearly separated from Versicolor and Virginica.
Versicolor and Virginica exhibit some overlap in their measurements.
A few minor outliers are visible in the box plots, but no major outliers are present.
Petal-related features provide better separation among species than sepal-related features.
